Source Tags & Codes - ...And You Will Know Us By The Trail Of Dead

2013-02-26

Hard to truly know what to say about this one. Trail Of Dead’s masterful “Source Tags & Codes” was an album that really opened some doors for me. It was the first time I realized just how much good music there is that I’ve never heard of. That was powerful.

It was that first taste; once you’ve had it, you can’t continue to eat white bread and feel good about it.

Sounds like: scratchy, grainy, imperfect, real
